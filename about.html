I am currently an undergraduate student at the University of Miami. I was born in India and live in Orlando Fl. My hobbies include outdoor activities such as tennis, basketball, table tennis and biking. I also enjoy the fine arts through music, playing the tabla, reading and writing.

I have come to realize that many times we attribute our success to our own efforts and grind, however what we fail to realize is that we are mere beneficiaries in a greater scheme of chance that allowed us to achieve what we have.

Hence, I strive to take the opportunities that were given to to me, to create even more opportunities for the people and communities around myself.
