Introduction

The tabla is an Indian percussion instrument that is used in Indian classical and folk music. I have been learning the tabla for over 10 years now, and I decided a few years back to merge the Indian folk sound of the tabla with American popular music. I have created multiple tabla covers to many famous songs you may see below.
